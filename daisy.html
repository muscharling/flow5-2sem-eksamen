    // LÆR AT LAVE DAISY-ØRERINGE

    At lære at lave sine egne smykker, behøver ikke være svært - Se vores tutorials nedenfor. Be aware that the coatings will eventually wear off. This is natural and not a reason for complaint . But if you treat your jewelry properly , they will last longer – Therefore: Avoid your jewelry to be in direct contact with creams , perfumes orother cosmetics. Take your jewelry off when you sleep, (sun ) bathing, swimming pool or sports . Avoid storing your jewelry in the bathroom, although it looks nice.



    // MATERIALER

    Til disse Daisy-øreringe skal du bruge:

    • Japanske Miyuki-perler
    • Polyester perle-tråd
    • Lukket O-ring
    • Klem-perle
    • Hoops
    • Saks
    • Perler split-nål
