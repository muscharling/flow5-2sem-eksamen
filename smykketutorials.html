    // ALLE SMYKKETUTORIALS

    At lære at lave sine egne smykker, behøver ikke være svært - Se vores tutorials nedenfor. Be aware that the coatings will eventually wear off. This is natural and not a reason for complaint . But if you treat your jewelry properly , they will last longer – Therefore: Avoid your jewelry to be in direct contact with creams , perfumes orother cosmetics. Take your jewelry off when you sleep, (sun ) bathing, swimming pool or sports . Avoid storing your jewelry in the bathroom, although it looks nice. Enjoy!
